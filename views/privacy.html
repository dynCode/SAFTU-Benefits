PRIVACY POLICY

Welcome to SAFTU Benefit Services (Pty) Ltd (Registration number 2019/198364/07) privacy policy. SAFTU Benefit Services and all of its direct or indirect subsidiaries and affiliates respect your privacy and take the protection of personal information very seriously.

SAFTU Benefit Services will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party at any time.

    1. SAFTU Benefit Services will use your personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected or agreed with you, for example:
      1. To provide our products or services to you, to carry out the transaction you requested and to maintain our relationship with you;
      2. For underwriting purposes;
      3. To assess and process claims;
      4. To confirm and verify your identity or to verify that you are an authorised user for security purposes;
      5. For purposes of claim checks (e.g. Claims Register);
      6. For the detection and prevention of fraud, crime, money laundering or other malpractice;
      7. To conduct market or customer satisfaction research or for statistical analysis;
      8. For audit and record keeping purposes;
      9. In connection with legal proceedings.
    2. SAFTU Benefit Services will disclose information when lawfully required to do so
      1. to comply with any relevant legislation,
      2. or any legal process; and
      3. by any regulatory authority (like the Financial Services Board) and
      4. the Regulators it may appoint for the various financial and private sectors.

    1. SAFTU Benefit Services are legally obliged to provide adequate protection for the personal information SAFTU Benefit Services hold about our members and to stop unauthorised access and use of personal information. SAFTU Benefit Services intends to protect the quality and integrity of your personal information. SAFTU Benefit Services has implemented appropriate technical and organizational information security measures (including, but not limited to, using encryption and secured logins) to help keep your information secure, accurate, current, and complete.
    2. Our security policies and procedures cover:
      1. Computer, network and system security;
      2. Access to personal information;
      3. Secure communications;
      4. Security in contracting out activities or functions;
      5. Retention and disposal of information;
      6. Acceptable usage of personal information;
      7. Governance and regulatory issues;
      8. Monitoring access and usage of private information;
      9. Investigating and reacting to security incidents.
    3. SAFTU Benefit Services may share aggregated information (for example, demographic data) with its shareholders and business partners, but it will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent or as provided in this privacy statement.
    4. SAFTU Benefit Services may need to disclose personal information to our employees that require the personal information to do their jobs. These include our responsible management department, human resources department, accounting department, audit, compliance department, information technology, and or our Call Centre agents and employees.
